I'm Ashutosh — a full-stack developer in the making, with a tendency to ask more questions than I answer. I'm drawn to the craft of building on the web: the logic of it, the aesthetics of it, the way a well-made interface disappears and lets the work speak.

Outside the editor, I spend a lot of time with books, philosophy, and the occasional thought that insists on being written down. I believe good work — whether code or prose — starts with thinking carefully about what you're actually trying to do.

This site is a living document. Projects I've built, things I've read, thoughts I've had. Still early. Always in progress.
